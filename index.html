Schedule

Transcend will be of three rounds

Round One

The aspirant will have to attend a General Aptitude and Problem Solving Test after which eligible aspirants will move on to the next round

Round Two

All eligible aspirants will have to attend a Company Specified test or an Advanced Test based on the genre opted by the candidate

Round Three

Aspirants who sucessfully complete Round Three of Transcend gets to attend an interview by the employer or the jury panel
